Japanese Beef Curry

Check Home for more recipes

Description

Japanese beef curry rice bowl is delicious. But, is it easy to make? The answer is, it's as easy as finishing a Japanese beef curry rice bowl!

Ingredients

  • Diced Beef Braising Steak x400g
  • carrot x1
  • Potatoes x2
  • Onion x1
  • Japanese curry mix x1 package

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients

    2. Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Sauté

    3. Heat a little oil in a large pot. Add the diced beef and onions, and sauté until lightly browned. Add potatoes and carrots, and stir briefly.

  3. Simmer

    4. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Skim off any foam. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for about 30-40 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.

  4. Add Japanese curry mix

    5. Turn off the heat. Break the curry mix blocks into smaller pieces and stir into the pot until completely dissolved.

  5. Thicken the curry

    6. Turn the heat back on low. Stir occasionally and let it simmer for another 5-10 minutes until the sauce thickens. Adjust flavor with soy sauce, oyster sauce, chilli flakes if desired.

  6. Serve

    7. Spoon curry over a plate of hot white rice. Enjoy the warm, rich, and comforting Japanese beef curry rice!
