Korean Noodle Soup

Check Home for more recipes

Description

Korea noodle soup is easy to make. Basicly you put a pack of Korean noodle in a pot of boiled water together with the seasoning bags that come with it. That's it. You make it!

To make it more nutritious, put in eggs, vegetable and any kind of meat you like.

There is no strict recipe to follow. Make it as you wish! You decide your own recipe!

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients I usally use to make my own Korean noodle soup(but as I've said, no strict recipe. You can always freestyle with your own ingredients!):

  • Korean noodle pack(come with 2 seasoning bags in side) x1
  • Egg x2
  • Tomato x2
  • Spring oinion x2
  • Red chilli x3(I love spicy! No need to put chilli if you can't eat spicy!)

You can also put meat in. Here are the meat which I like to put in:

  • Bacon
  • Hot dog sussage

Steps

I usually make my korean noddle like this:

  1. Chop the tomatoes, spring onions, bacon, hot dog sussage or any other ingredients you'd like to put into small dices
  2. Boil a kettle of water(about 500ml) so you have boiled water to be aside
  3. Heat up the wok, put a tea spoon of olive oil in and fry 1 egg
  4. When the egg is fried into a fixed shape, put the chopped ingredients. Stir fry them while the fried egg is aside
  5. Pour the boied into the wok until the water covers all the ingredients and the water level is about one finger tip above
  6. Crack another egg in and let it boil in the water for
  7. Open the korean noodle pack, put the noodle and the seasoning bags in. If the water can't cover the noodle, put more hot water in.
  8. Boil everything for 3 to 5 min
  9. Put some sesame oil in before you get everything in the bowl. It will taste and smell even better!
  10. That's it. Go enjoy it and make sure eat slowly and safe as the noodle soup can be very hot!
